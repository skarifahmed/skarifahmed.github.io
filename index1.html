About Me

Dr. Arif Ahmed currently serves as a Senior Researcher at the BioAI Lab, UiT The Arctic University of Norway. His prior academic appointments include Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, XIM University (2021–2024), and Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Physics and Technology at UiT (2019–2021). He was previously an Assistant Professor of Computer Applications at Haldia Institute of Technology, India (2009–2019).

He earned his Ph.D. at the National Institute of Technology Durgapur, following undergraduate studies at Burdwan University. His research spans Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence, with interdisciplinary applications in computer science, physics, and biology. Dr. Ahmed holds consultant positions with the Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST) and IIT Bhubaneswar and is a Senior Member of IEEE and Digital Life Norway (DLN).
